        A vulture
        B foot
        C pool (rectangle)
        D hand
        E sail
        F viper
        G jar stand
        H twisted wick
        I reed
        J [not present]
        K basket with handle
        L lion
        M owl
        N water
        O drooping plant
        P mat (square)
        Q [not present]
        R mouth
        S door bolt
        T bread (semicircle)
        U rope
        V mouth with vertical stroke
        W chick
        X foot in boot?
        Y hoe (?)
        Z [not present]

Then, reverse each block of six letters.
