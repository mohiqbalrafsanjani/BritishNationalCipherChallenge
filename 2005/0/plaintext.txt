MOST SECRET
MARCH 18TH 1959
TO CAPTAIN OF THE K3 SUBMARINE
YOU ARE INSTRUCTED TO LEAVE YOUR HOME PORT AT ONCE AND PROCEED
TO THE CRASH SITE OF THE BEACON WHILE THERE IS EVIDENCE THAT
THE US HAVE SOURCES WITHIN THE WASHINGTON EMBASSY THE FULL
SIGNIFICANCE OF PROJECT TRIANGULATE IS PROBABLY NOT KNOWN TO
THEM IF WE ARE LUCKY THEY WILL NEGLECT THE CRASH SITE AND WE
WILL RETRIEVE THE BEACON WITHOUT FURTHER INCIDENT
