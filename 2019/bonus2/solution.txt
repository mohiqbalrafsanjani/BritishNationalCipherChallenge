Type 4 periodic polyalphabetic substitution cipher, also called Quagmire 4

plaintext alphabet:  natiolbcdefghjkmpqrsuvwxyz
ciphertext alphabet: CIPHERABDFGJKLMNOQSTUVWXYZ
shift key:           CHALLENGE

So the keywords are NATIONAL CIPHER CHALLENGE

The solution to the math problem is "PAGE FIFTY NINE TO PAGE NINETY TWO"
(there are other mathematically correct answers, but note that if you
remove an odd-numbered page, you must also remove the even-numbered page
on the other side of it)
