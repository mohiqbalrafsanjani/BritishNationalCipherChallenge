The back story

The nineteenth century scientists Babbage, Lovelace and Wheatstone have designed a new and revolutionary cipher machine, seventy five years ahead of their time. To their dismay the plans were stolen by a freelance crook, who had threatened to pass them to the erstwhile enemy of the British, the French, with whom the country has estbalished an uneasy peace. In a dramatic chase the villain fell to his death from The Monument, and the plans were recovered. Believing the machine and its design to be safe our friends have returned to a more leisurely life, but the matter has not yet reached its conclusion ...
